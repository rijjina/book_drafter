# Document Types And Quality Targets

## ตำรา

A ตำรา is an academic work used for teaching an entire course or part of a course. It analyzes, synthesizes, compiles, and organizes theory, research, and studied knowledge around a central concept with coherent related concepts. It must use academic language, be current at submission, identify the related course, and be readable independently.

Expected components include a preface, contents, main text, explanation or analysis, summaries, citations, bibliography, and index. Data, diagrams, examples, and cases should make the main ideas independently understandable.

## หนังสือ

A หนังสือ studies a topic comprehensively and deeply, analyzes and synthesizes it systematically, and organizes central and related concepts coherently. It uses academic language and presents the author's viewpoint in a way that strengthens the field. It need not follow one course or curriculum.

Expected components include a preface, contents, main text, analysis, summaries, citations, bibliography, and index. An authored book must have unity and a sound academic foundation. A set of book chapters must remain coherent, independently understandable, methodologically sound, and clear about each contributor's role.

## Quality Level B

Require all of the following:

- academically correct, complete, and current content;
- clear concepts and presentation;
- value to higher-education teaching for a ตำรา, or value to the academic field for a หนังสือ.

## Quality Level A

Require Level B plus:

- analysis and presentation of knowledge or methods current with academic progress;
- original ideas and relevant author experience or research;
- high academic detail and depth;
- potential for broad national or international academic use and citation.

## Quality Level A+

Require Level A plus:

- synthesis that creates a new body of knowledge or theory;
- sustained stimulation of further thought and research, evidenced by broad international citation;
- international trust and recognition in the relevant academic or professional community.

## Publication And Evidence

For academic-rank use, verify the current institution's requirements for peer review, publication method, breadth of dissemination, ISBN/bibliographic information, and evidence dates. Do not infer that a finished manuscript satisfies publication requirements.

## Reuse Restriction

Work previously submitted as เอกสารประกอบการสอน or เอกสารคำสอน generally cannot simply be relabeled as ตำรา or หนังสือ. A ตำรา may be developed from teaching documents only when the development is clearly substantial and the governing rules allow it.

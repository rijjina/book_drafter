# Teaching Document Criteria

This reference distills the 2022 Chiang Mai University regulation bundled as `ประกาศประเมินการสอน.pdf`. Use the current institution's regulation when supplied.

## Document Distinction

### เอกสารประกอบการสอน

An academic work used to support the evaluation of teaching in a course under the university curriculum. It reflects course content and teaching methods systematically and serves as an important instructor tool.

### เอกสารคำสอน

An academic work used to teach a course under the university curriculum. It reflects the content and teaching methods systematically, may be developed from เอกสารประกอบการสอน, is more complete, and serves as an important learner tool for self-study or further study.

## Use Evidence

The regulation states that the produced document should follow the applicant's teaching assignment and have been used for at least one academic term. For trimester systems, the cited rule also describes a minimum teaching load of three credits or 45 hours across the relevant terms. Verify the current local wording before asserting eligibility.

## Eight Evaluation Criteria

1. **Correctness and currency of content**: theories, formulas, rules, experiments, data, interpretations, evidence, citations, and terminology are academically correct and currently accepted.
2. **Course coverage**: covers every essential topic in the portion of the course for which the author is responsible.
3. **Content sequence**: presents teaching content in an order that supports understanding.
4. **Writing format**: communicates a clear line of thought and explanation, uses helpful media where appropriate, cites academic sources lawfully, and includes a reference plan, bibliography, and suitable appendices.
5. **Research and source study**: demonstrates broad study of textbooks, books, documents, and research that helps learners deepen knowledge and continue searching.
6. **Language correctness**: uses organized Thai syntax, standardized terms, and technically correct vocabulary.
7. **Author's own ideas**: includes additional opinion, critique, recommendations, application, conclusions, or new insights arising from analysis and synthesis.
8. **Value of the document**: is important, credible, useful for teaching or further study, and suitable for citation.

## Scoring

Score each item:

- `4` = ดีมาก
- `3` = ดี
- `2` = พอใช้
- `1` = ควรปรับปรุง

Calculate the mean of all eight items:

- `3.26-4.00` = ดีมาก
- `2.51-3.25` = ดี
- `<= 2.50` = ควรปรับปรุง

The cited regulation requires the document evaluation to be at least `ดี`. Treat any criterion with missing evidence, serious factual error, plagiarism/copyright risk, or failed course coverage as a blocker regardless of the arithmetic mean.

## Internal A-Equivalent Production Target

This skill uses a stricter internal target when preparing เอกสารคำสอน:

- mean score `3.26-4.00`;
- every criterion at least `3`;
- no blockers;
- complete evidence of current scholarship, high depth, broad research/source study, relevant author experience, and identifiable analysis/synthesis.

Do not describe `A-equivalent` as an official grade or institutional decision.
